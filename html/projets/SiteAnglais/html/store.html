Our History

We make our own chocolate since 1861, Jean Lapierre founded his store with his wife Sophie Lapierre. His idea was to make good chocolate with noble products. Jean supported local producters and only used quality cocoa. After giving the store to his children, the Lapierre Chocolate Manufacture kept this idea in mind. Today, we only use products from France and biologic cocoa.
